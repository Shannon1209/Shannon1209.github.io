Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2025

Testimony Jar respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, and protected when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. All testimonies and mood data are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

Your testimonies and emotional data are used solely to provide you with a personalized experience within the app. No data is shared with third parties or stored on external servers.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. You have full control and can delete or export your data at any time from the app settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell any user data. The app operates entirely offline.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time. Since no data is stored on our servers, uninstalling the app will remove all your testimonies and mood information.

6. Children's Privacy

Testimony Jar is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
